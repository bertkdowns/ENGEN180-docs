Engineering Basics
==================

What is Engineering?
--------------------

Technology is tools.

Science is research.

Engineering is using research to make tools

Traits of a good Engineer
-------------------------

	- Interpersonal skills - we work in groups
	- Communication skills - we need to talk to groups/clients
	- Continuing education - stay up to date, learn skills for specific task
	- Maintain a professional libray - know where to find helpful info
	- Curiosity of nature & machines & processes - ideas
	- Organisation - people, budget
	- Honesty - trust
	- Dependability - need to be relied on
	- Creativity - unique ideas
		
		- Analogies
		- Visualisation
		- Think outside the discipline
